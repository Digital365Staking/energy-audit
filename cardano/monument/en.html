Cardano’s Midnight Project & Monument Bank Partnership

Cardano’s Midnight project is making a major move into traditional finance through a strategic partnership with Monument Bank, a UK-regulated digital bank. This collaboration aims to bring real-world banking services onto blockchain infrastructure.

Monument Bank plans to tokenize up to £250 million in customer deposits, marking one of the first large-scale integrations of blockchain into retail banking.

What Are Tokenized Deposits?

Tokenized deposits are digital representations of traditional bank savings held on a blockchain. Each token corresponds directly to real money stored in the bank.

These deposits remain fully backed, redeemable in GBP, and protected under existing financial regulations—combining the safety of traditional banking with the flexibility of blockchain.

Privacy Meets Compliance

Midnight leverages zero-knowledge proofs to ensure transaction privacy while still meeting regulatory requirements. This allows banks to adopt blockchain technology without exposing sensitive financial data.

A Three-Phase Financial Evolution

The partnership will roll out in three phases:

  • Tokenized savings deposits
  • Access to tokenized investment products
  • Lending against tokenized assets
This approach could democratize access to financial products traditionally reserved for high-net-worth individuals.

Why This Matters

This partnership signals a major shift in how financial systems may evolve. By combining blockchain technology with regulated banking, Cardano’s Midnight project is helping bridge the gap between Web3 and traditional finance.

If successful, it could unlock billions in on-chain financial activity and pave the way for broader institutional adoption.
