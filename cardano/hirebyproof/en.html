🚀 HireByProof

Verifiable Talent Marketplace — Proof of Concept (Starting June 2026)

Starting in June 2026, we will develop a Proof of Concept for a new type of hiring platform designed to improve how companies identify, evaluate, and hire talent.

This solution replaces CV-based hiring with project-based, evidence-driven selection.

💡 The Concept

Companies don’t just post job descriptions — they publish real tasks or projects.

  • Automatic candidate matching
  • Evaluation based on proven past work
  • Optional real task execution

Hiring becomes based on what candidates have done — and can do.

⚙️ How It Works

1. Task & Project-Based Hiring

  • Real tasks and missions
  • Required skills and tech stack
  • Expected outcomes and constraints
  • Budget or hiring intent

2. Verifiable Candidate Profiles

  • Verified project history
  • Proven contributions
  • Skill attestations

3. Intelligent Matching

  • Project similarity analysis
  • Skill overlap
  • Experience level matching
  • Reputation signals

4. Hire Through Execution

  • Select candidates directly
  • Invite to complete tasks
  • Hire based on real performance
“Try before you hire” becomes the standard.

🔐 Privacy-First Architecture

  • Proof of work without exposing sensitive data
  • Selective disclosure of experience
  • Privacy-preserving identity

🧪 Proof of Concept Scope

  • Project posting system
  • Candidate profiles with proofs
  • Verification mechanism
  • Matching engine
  • Task-based evaluation flow

🎯 Expected Outcomes

  • Faster hiring decisions
  • Higher-quality candidate matching
  • Reduced reliance on CVs and interviews

🧭 Vision

A new category of hiring platform where talent is selected based on verified work and real execution, not self-reported experience.
