🚀 HireByProof

Verifiable Talent Marketplace — Proof of Concept (Starting October 2026)

Starting in October 2026, we will develop a Proof of Concept for a new type of hiring platform designed to improve how companies identify, evaluate, and hire talent.

This solution replaces CV-based hiring with project-based, evidence-driven selection.

🔗 Blockchain Integration (Cardano + Midnight)

The platform leverages a hybrid blockchain architecture combining Cardano and Midnight to deliver verifiable, privacy-preserving hiring workflows.

1. Cardano Layer (Public Verification & Anchoring)

  • Immutable storage of proof hashes for candidate work history
  • Timestamping of project completions and attestations
  • Smart contracts (Plutus) for validation logic
  • Auditability for companies and third parties
Cardano ensures that proofs cannot be altered or forged once recorded.

2. Midnight Layer (Privacy & Selective Disclosure)

  • Confidential candidate profiles
  • Selective disclosure of skills and project experience
  • Zero-knowledge proofs (ZKPs) for validating claims
  • Protection of sensitive employer and project data
Candidates prove their experience without revealing confidential details.

3. Proof of Work Flow

Project Completed → Contribution Verified → Proof Generated (Midnight) → Hash Anchored on Cardano → Reusable Verifiable Credential

4. Matching Engine Integration

The matching engine consumes verified data from both layers:

  • Midnight → private skill and experience proofs
  • Cardano → public verification and integrity checks

This enables trusted candidate ranking without exposing unnecessary personal data.

5. Smart Contract Use Cases

  • Proof validation and attestation signing
  • Reputation scoring updates
  • Optional milestone-based payments for tasks
  • Escrow for task-based hiring workflows

🧭 Vision

A new category of hiring platform where talent is selected based on verified work and real execution, not self-reported experience.
