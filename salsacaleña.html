🇬🇧 Salsa Caleña Shows Across Europe

A Madrid-based troupe sharing the passion of Salsa Caleña throughout Europe

We are a dance troupe based in Madrid, specialized in Salsa Caleña shows performed throughout Europe. Through our performances, we aim to share and promote the richness of Colombian culture, known for its warmth, energy and joyful spirit.

Our show celebrates the greatest classics of Salsa Caleña, combining dynamic choreography, a festive atmosphere and the passion of this iconic musical tradition from Cali, Colombia.

A colorful experience inviting audiences to discover the vibrant and joyful world of Colombian salsa.

Our Show

Guided by the rhythms of Colombian percussion, with precise movements, artistic elegance and the contagious energy of our dancers, our troupe offers a unique cultural journey into the heart of Colombia. Each performance celebrates the history, passion and festive spirit of Salsa Caleña.
